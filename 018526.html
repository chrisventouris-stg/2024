Hosted Holidays Escorted Tours Tailor-Made
 
 
  Experience is...perfectly planned, expertly led adventures
 
If you're looking to get under the skin of somewhere and enjoy authentic experiences in a more intimate setting – our small group tours are ideal.
Our carefully crafted itineraries are designed for group sizes fewer than 24, so you can visit smaller, out-of-the-way places which aren’t often accessible to those in larger groups. You can also stay in characterful accommodation close to key locations, in-keeping with natural surroundings. An eco-lodge in Peru’s Amazon rainforest is great for nocturnal jungle walks, for instance, and a peaceful guesthouse in Bhutan’s Punakha Valley is perfect for strolling along the Puna Tsang Chu River flanked by rice paddy seas.
Thanks to the size of the group, you can get up much closer to real life and maybe spot the Big Five on all-day game drives or meet local people on village visits in Kenya’s Masai Mara National Reserve. You’ll also find it much easier to form closer bonds with fellow travellers and have more time with expert tour guides who can recommend where to go and what to do on free days.
In fact, local recommendations are a guarantee with all Saga tours alongside included international flights, and getting picked up and dropped off back home again with our VIP travel service.
