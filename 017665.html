Experience is… sightseeing in Sydney
Australia’s largest city is full of icons – from Sydney Opera House to Bondi Beach. Other big draws include the stunning coastline, world-class restaurants, and vibrant arts scene. Sydney is also a great springboard for natural beauty spots like the Blue Mountains.

Experience is… exploring The Great Barrier Reef
The world’s largest coral reef system needs little introduction. Famous for its extraordinary marine life and rich biodiversity, it's a UNESCO World Heritage site and natural wonder. Snorkel beneath the waves or enjoy a catamaran ride or helicopter flight from above.

Experience is… coming face to face with Uluru (Ayers Rock)
Uluru, a massive sandstone monolith in Australia's Northern Territory, holds deep cultural significance for the Indigenous Anangu people. Its striking red colour and sacred history make it a must-visit when travelling in the Land Down Under.
