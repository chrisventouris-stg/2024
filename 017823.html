Scenic Lake Orta and a Matterhorn Rail Journey
7‍ n‍ights from £1,‍9‍3‍5pp
This week’s holiday of the week combines idyllic Italian lakes with spectacular Swiss landscapes: Scenic Lake Orta and a Matterhorn Rail Journey.
In Italy, we’ll explore the untouched beauty of Lake Orta. Cobblestone streets. Traditional trattorias. Take a boat trip across glistening waters. We’ll also roam Lake Maggiore’s pretty towns and botanical gardens, with plenty of free time to enjoy it all at our own pace.
As an added bonus, we’ll cross over into stunning Switzerland and jump on board the famous Bernina Express, as we’re rewarded with jaw dropping Alpine views – straight off a chocolate box – and travel through 55 tunnels and over 196 bridges. Seeing really is believing…
