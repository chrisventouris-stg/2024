  What if… you could discover Canada & Alaska your way
 
Mother Nature had a field day with Canada and Alaska. Picture snowy mountain peaks giving way to storybook forests and sparkling glaciers dipping down to iceberg-flecked fjords. This is also a haven for wildlife lovers – moose, grizzly bears and bald eagles are just a few of the iconic species you can encounter here.
Jet across the pond with our trusted partner, Air Canada – it’s then up to you how you want to explore. Hit the highway on a self-drive road trip, enjoy an immersive independent tour, or pair time exploring towns and cities with a luxury cruise. See the full round-up below and call our travel consultants to book your 2025 Canadian adventure on 0808 134 3143.
