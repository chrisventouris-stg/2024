  What if… the tour was just the beginning?
 
Adding on a cruise to your tour gives you the best of both types of holiday. On the tour, you’ll enjoy insightful, immersive experiences with a brilliant group of people. Step aboard your cruise and you’ll have a port-packed itinerary to look forward to, plus fantastic onboard entertainment and amenities.
When it comes to cruise lines, we’re always looking for companies that can match our high standards of service and quality – that’s why we partner with Holland America Line for so many of our Cruise & Tour holidays.
The ships are stylish, the cabins comfortable and the crew warm and welcoming. And as for the locations, if the fjords of Alaska don’t tempt you, there’s also New England’s seaside towns, New Zealand’s waterfalls and wineries, or the idyllic islands of the Caribbean.
