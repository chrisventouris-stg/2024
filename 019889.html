WHATEVER YOUR INTEREST,
WE HAVE THE PERFECT HOLIDAY FOR YOU…
We are excited to introduce our 2026 Special Interest Holidays collection. These special holidays bring like-minded people together in some of our favourite hotels and offer the opportunity to learn new skills or indulge old passions. Ideal for beginners and experts alike – our award-winning holidays are led by an expert host and include at least four handpicked experiences – such as cooking demonstrations, workshops or evenings under star-filled skies.
 
 
BIRDWATCHING
Join an experienced host to observe a variety of birds in their natural habitat, including forest, marshland and riverbank. Enjoy the great outdoors as you seek out endemic species and rare sightings in a choice of destination, including Cyprus, Spain and Portugal.
Find out more
 
 
WALKING
Experience a unique blend of adventure, exercise and cultural immersion in a relaxed and friendly environment. Get closer to nature in a choice of destination from the mountains of Montenegro and Italy, levadas of Madeira, volcanic terrain of Gran Canaria and Lanzarote or the coast of Spain. You can even walk the Santiago trail.
Find out more
 
 
GARDENS
Explore natural scenery, discover endemic flora and visit an array of gardens in a choice of destinations, including Spain, Italy, Madeira and Tenerife. Admire the immaculate grounds of historic villas, immerse yourself in an exotic oasis filled with palms, or stroll through Laurissilva rainforests, every holiday includes a selection of excursions.
Find out more
 
 
FOOD AND WINE
Enjoy diverse culinary traditions in a choice of destinations, from Spain and Malta to Italy and Crete. Look forward to culinary-themed visits to local markets; cookery classes, demonstrations and workshops; vineyard tours; and the opportunity to sample delicious local specialities in the places they were produced.
Find out more
 
 
OTHER INTERESTS
We offer a variety of other special interest holidays too. Perhaps explore ancient wonders on an archaeology holiday. Admire medieval and modern masterpieces on an art holiday. You may like to learn a new skill or indulge a lifelong passion on a holiday focused on astronomy, nature and conservation, or bridge. You could even learn a new language.
Find out more
