Have you considered Portugal for your next holiday? It is rich with history, beautiful scenery, proud traditions and delicious cuisine to match. Choose a hotel stay on the sun-kissed Algarve and enjoy the fabulous beaches and scenery this famous coast has to offer (we even have birdwatching holidays if you wish). Or join one of our escorted tours which showcase everything from signature sights and cities like Lisbon to its quieter, more rustic side, such as the slow, vine-clad curves of the lovely Douro Valley. With so many different experiences on offer, and savings on selected holidays too, we are sure to have your perfect holiday.

Go off the tourist track
 
Our Grand Designs of Portugal tour goes inland, skirting the tranquil Serra da Estrela mountains and staying in historic converted hotels. Or if you want to spend your days exploring historic wineries and medieval towns, head for the peaceful Douro Valley.

Try Portuguese flavours
 
From fresh grilled sardines to the ubiquitous pasteis de nata (custard tarts) Portugal has many traditional flavours and speciality dishes on the menu. Read our blog to discover the fifteen foods we recommend you try during your holiday.
 
Find out more
 
 
 
See more of the Algarve
 
The Algarve famously offers fabulous beaches and golf courses, but there is so much more to discover, from rich traditions found in its music, festivals and cuisine, to historic cities and incredible rock formations sculpted by the wind and sea.
