India is a country that has dazzled visitors for decades. It’s got it all: vibrant cities, beautiful beaches, soul-stirring mountains, wildlife-rich jungle. There’s history and culture, religion and spirituality, and some of the tastiest food on the planet. There’s always a celebration or festival taking place – and visitors are more than welcome to join.
It’s a big place, but having an experienced tour manager or local guide by your side gives you the confidence to actively enjoy everything you’re seeing and doing (as Race Across the World’s Steven and Viv found out recently). Travel independently or with a group, by rail or road, even combine India with Nepal or Bhutan. Dive in and discover what India has to offer (and don’t forget to enter our competition to win a holiday there)…
