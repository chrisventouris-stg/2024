  Experience South Africa with Tailor-Made Travel
 
What if you could experience South Africa on a customisable Tailor-Made holiday. You could see the Big Five on safari, watch whales on a self-drive road trip, and relive the history of the KwaZulu-Natal Battlefields. And don’t forget cosmopolitan Cape Town, and the undulating West Cape wine estates with their gourmet restaurants and charming Dutch-colonial towns like Stellenbosch.
Also, there’s something to do all-year-round with winter – May to September – best for safaris and summer – December to March – ideal for touring the Cape Peninsula’s affluent seaside suburbs.
Read on for inspiration or call 0808 134 3143 to book your next Tailor‍-‍Made holiday in South Africa.

Exceptional safaris
Seeing the Big Five on safari is unforgettable. You’ll stay in an all-inclusive lodge with luxury tents, spa services, and a dining deck overlooking a flood-lit water hole. Twice daily game drives and guided bush walks are included. Just remember your camera, walking shoes, and khaki-coloured clothing.
Take me there
 
 
Wine estates and gourmet delights
Visit the vineyard covered mountain foothills, just outside of Cape Town, and enjoy a gourmet getaway in the historic towns of Stellenbosch and Franschhoek. A great idea is to combine a tour of the Cape Winelands with a city break in Cape Town or a self-drive along the Garden Route.
Take me there
 
 
Self-drive in South Africa
South Africa is an accessible country for a road trip. They drive on the left, signposts are in English, and you can drop off the hire car before going on safari. This gives you the freedom to explore the Cape Winelands, see whales from Hermanus, and travel to the historic KwaZulu-Natal Battlefields.
Take me there
 
 
Come to Cape Town
From cable cars up Table Mountain and world-class shopping and restaurants on the V&A Waterfront to tours to Cape Point via chic seaside suburbs and Boulders Beach penguins – Cape Town is the perfect place to start a self-drive and safari tour or even a twin-centre trip to Mauritius.
