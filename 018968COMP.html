  What if… the soothing clatter of a toy train was your soundtrack to adventure?
 
Sorry, you didn’t win the trip to India this time… but the adventure is far from over. While you didn’t scoop the prize in our recent competition for a trip for two to India, there’s still a whole world of wonder waiting for you. And if you're anything like Race Across the World’s Steven and Viv, one journey might just lead to a lifelong love affair with this incredible country. Delightfully diverse, wonderfully chaotic, and utterly mesmerising — India is a destination that captures hearts and souls.
Our expertly led India tours capture the essence of this vast country. Encounter hilltop forts, opulent palaces, and ancient temples at every twist and turn. Be starstruck by the romance of the Taj Mahal. Tune in to the rhythmic clatter of train wheels on the Kalka-Shimla Toy Train, winding through jagged peaks and lush pine forests. Search for Bengal tigers, leopards, sloth bears, and monkeys in Ranthambore National Park. Glide through the serene backwaters of Kerala. And marvel at the spectacular scenery of the forest-cloaked Himalayan foothills.
