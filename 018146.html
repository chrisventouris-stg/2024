Escorted Tours Hosted Holidays Tailor-Made
 
 
  What if… 2025 was full of adventure?
 
With 2025 fast approaching, now is the perfect time to start planning your next getaway. Whether you’re after an immersive tour or a laid-back hosted holiday, we’ve got something for you. Here’s a taste of the incredible destinations waiting for you in 2025.
Find out more
 
 
Costa Rica
Enjoy a fabulous rooftop terrace, infinity-edge pool (one of two) and a selection of bars, lounges and restaurants. You’re less than ten minutes’ walk from a rocky beach, too.
Find out more
 
 
Spain
Sunny Spain has it all. Lose yourself in Barcelona’s vibrant streets, stroll through the history-rich lanes of Andalusia, or stretch out on the golden shores of the Costa Brava. Our escorted tours take you to cultural icons like the Alhambra and Park Güell, while our hosted holidays let you soak in the Spanish lifestyle at your own pace.
Find out more
 
 
Japan
A country of contrasts, Japan blends the ancient and the ultramodern like nowhere else. Discover Kyoto’s serene temples, Tokyo’s neon buzz, and the iconic beauty of Mount Fuji on our escorted tours. From traditional tea ceremonies to cutting-edge technology, this is a land where every moment feels extraordinary.
Find out more
 
 
Italy
Italy is a feast for the senses. Sip espresso in a Venetian café, admire the grandeur of the Colosseum, or bask in Tuscany’s golden sunsets. Guided tours bring history to life with visits to the Vatican, Pompeii, and Pisa, while hosted holidays let you slow down and savour la dolce vita in specially selected, family-run hotels.
Find out more
