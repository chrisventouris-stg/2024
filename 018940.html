  What if… you could experience Sri Lanka?
 
Teardrop-shaped Sri Lanka may be small, but this Indian Ocean isle has so much to offer. It’s blessed with untouched beaches and diverse landscapes, which range from lush jungles to sprawling tea plantations. In this so-called ‘Land of Serendipity’, you can spot whales, track leopards, and explore holy sights like Adam’s Peak – a mountain and pilgrimage site said to have the sacred footprint of Buddha.
