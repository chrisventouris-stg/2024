  Experience is… perfectly planned, expertly led adventures
 
With picture postcard lakes and snow-dusted mountains in the north and glamorous coasts, riviera towns and sun-kissed Sicily in the south, it’s little wonder Italy is a best-selling destination. The flights aren’t too long, the weather is wonderful, and - alongside the scenery - Italy’s cities, art, architecture and cuisine are among the best in the world.
A tour is the best way to experience all this and with Saga you can relax knowing we’ve planned the route, handpicked the hotels and included a range of excursions like guided tours, boat trips, train rides and food tastings. From cruising on Lake Orta and exploring 1,000-year-old cave dwellings in Matera to seeing exquisite Byzantine mosaics in Palermo and tasting pasticcini biscuits in Erice, you’ll experience the best of each region, whichever tour you choose. Remember we also include your flights, travel insurance§ and our VIP door-to-door travel service, for unbeatable Saga value.
