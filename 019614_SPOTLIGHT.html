From the pin-up pretty lakes and snow-dusted mountains of the north to the glamorous coasts and sun-bleached cities in the south, Italy is one of Europe’s most seductive travel destinations. Who can resist Venice’s swoon-worthy canals and masterpiece palazzos, the spine-tingling history of sites like the Colosseum and Pompeii, or the film-set beauty of Sicily and Capri? Add celebrated food and wine, from the hearty bolognese to a classic seafood risotto and a bold red Barolo from Piedmont, and you have all the makings of a perfect holiday.
