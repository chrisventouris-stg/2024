  What if… you could have the best of both worlds?
 
The beauty of a Cruise & Stay holiday is that it offers double the excitement – time spent sailing the ocean blues combined with a spell relaxing on dry land. And we’ve partnered up with industry heavyweight, Azamara, to bring you a whole host of itineraries as part of our independent Tailor-Made Travel collection.
