  Experience is… celebrating the seasons
 
Have you booked your 2025 holiday yet? Time it right and you can add a seasonal twist to your trip. Here are our destination tips for the seasons ahead.
South Africa is glorious during our summertime, particularly for safaris in places like Kruger National Park, and wildflowers in the Western and Northern Cape. Closer to home, Italy never disappoints with places like Sicily and the Amalfi at their glamorous best in the high summer months. Heading into autumn we recommend India for comfortable daytime temperatures, cultural festivals and lush green landscapes after the monsoon rains. Japan is also a scenic treat in autumn with its renowned foliage displays.
Lots of us head to sunnier climes in winter, and Saga has a whole range of winter sunshine holidays to choose from. Popular options in Europe include Cyprus and Spain, but we also have tempting long-haul holidays in sunspots from Thailand to the Maldives.
