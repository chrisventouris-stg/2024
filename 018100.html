  What if… you could experience India?
 
When it comes to India, it’s impossible to fit everything into one trip. Covering more than 1.2 million square miles, you’ll find everything from steamy jungle to sand-dusted desert to snowy peaks – and much more besides.
We’ve mapped out more than a dozen escorted tours here, so there’s plenty to inspire your own trip. You might want to tour the classic Golden Triangle, ticking off icons like the Taj Mahal and Jaipur’s Palace of Winds. Or perhaps wildlife is your passion, in which case you’ll love spotting leopards and tigers in Central India’s reserves. There are temples and tea plantations in the sultry south; cool hill stations and toy trains in the north; and cities brimming with colour and chaos.
Each tour has been thoughtfully planned by our experts to ensure you’ll experience the very best of India. You might join a local family as they cook dinner or attend a spiritual ceremony at a temple or shrine – on some tours, you’ll even bed down in restored royal residences! And a tour manager will be on hand throughout, to keep things running smoothly and to help with anything you might need.
