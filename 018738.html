Our holiday of the week takes you to tropical shores in the Indian Ocean – we're putting Domaine de Grand Baie Residence in the spotlight, part of our independent travel collection.
This stylish retreat has contemporary good looks and an impressive list of facilities, including a gorgeous pool, a spa, and a fine dining restaurant.
